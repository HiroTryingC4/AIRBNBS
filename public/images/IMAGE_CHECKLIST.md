## 📷 Photography Tips

### Lighting
- ✅ Shoot during daytime with natural light
- ✅ Turn on all lights for evening shots
- ✅ Avoid harsh shadows
- ❌ Don't shoot in dim lighting

### Composition
- ✅ Keep camera level (use grid lines)
- ✅ Show the space, not just objects
- ✅ Include context (windows, doors, furniture)
- ✅ Shoot from corners to show more space
- ❌ Don't use extreme wide-angle (looks distorted)

### Staging
- ✅ Clean and declutter before shooting
- ✅ Arrange furniture neatly
- ✅ Add small decorative touches (pillows, plants)
- ✅ Open curtains for natural light
- ❌ Don't leave personal items visible

### Equipment
- 📱 Smartphone camera is fine (use portrait mode for depth)
- 📷 DSLR/Mirrorless camera is better
- 🎥 Tripod helps for stability
- 💡 Natural light is best

## 🎬 Video (Optional)

If you want to add a video tour:
- [ ] Record a 1-2 minute walkthrough
- [ ] Upload to YouTube or Vimeo
- [ ] Add the URL to `mockMediaGallery` in `lib/mockData.ts`

## ✅ Before You Shoot

- [ ] Clean the entire unit
- [ ] Remove personal items
- [ ] Charge your camera/phone
- [ ] Check lighting conditions
- [ ] Plan your shot list
- [ ] Shoot more than you need (you can select the best later)

## 📤 After Shooting

1. [ ] Select the best 15-20 photos
2. [ ] Crop to recommended dimensions
3. [ ] Compress images (use TinyPNG)
4. [ ] Rename files descriptively
5. [ ] Copy to appropriate folders
6. [ ] Update code with new URLs
7. [ ] Test on the website

---

**Pro Tip**: Take 3-5 shots of each area from different angles. You can choose the best one later!
